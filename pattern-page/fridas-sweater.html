Frida´s sweater

description

Frida´s sweater is knitted from the top down, in stockinette stick with twisted ribs.

The yoke is made from provisional cast on and continues with raglan increases.

The sweater has an elegant v-neck and collar which is made at the end from the provisional cast on.

Always start with a swatch to check the gauge.

The pattern is sent digitally as a pdf file

Degree of difficulty: 3 of 5

Info

Sizes: XS (S) M (L) XL (2XL)

Gauge: 4”/10 cm = 12 sts / 18 rows stockinette. Taken after wet blocking using 8 mm needles.

Needles: Circular needle size 8 mm - 40 cm and 60/80 cm. Circular needle size 7 mm ( US 10 ) - 40 cm and 60/80 cm and double pointed needle if you do not use magic loop.

Yarn: One thread Drops Brushed Alpaca Silk (25g = 140m) together with one thread Drops Muskat (50g = 100m)

Yarn options: One thread Sandnes Silk Mohair or Dale garn Kidsilk Erle together with one thread Sandnes Line eller Sandnes Duo or Gjestal garn Pure Merino or Dale garn Lerke

Materials:100 (100) 125 (150) 150 (200) g Drops brushed alpaca silk Lys Lavendel 17 together with 350 (350) 400 (450) 500 (500) g Drops muskat Lavendel 01

Measurements:
Length back: 50 (51) 53 (59) 60 (63) cm
Body: 110 (115) 120 (125) 135 (138) cm
